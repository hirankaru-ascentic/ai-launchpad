Before you apply

Is this for you?

Places are limited, and participants are selected through an application process. Apply only if both of these fit.

Who can apply

You're eligible if you're a…

  • 3rd or 4th year IT undergraduate
  • Recent IT graduate
  • From any university or institution
  • Ready to learn by doing — building as you go
What we'll ask of you

You can commit to…

  • Attending all 6 Saturday sessions
  • Spending real time developing your project
  • Working closely with your assigned mentor
  • Build a solution to a problem that interests you
  • Presenting at the Showcase if shortlisted

You'll be trained and supported throughout the journey — we'll be there for you every step of the way.

Good to know

Questions, answered.

How much time will it take each week?+
A 2-hour session every Saturday for 6 of the 8 weeks, plus your own time building your project and a weekly check-in with your mentor. The more you put in, the stronger your final project.
Do I need to be a strong coder?+
No. You need to be a 3rd/4th year IT undergrad or recent graduate who's willing to learn and build. The sessions teach you how to use AI to create value, and your mentor guides you through the process.
What exactly will I build?+
You'll identify a real-world problem worth solving and design, prototype, or build an AI-enabled solution or proof of concept around it. The idea is yours — the mentorship helps you make it real.
What happens if I'm shortlisted?+
Selected participants present their projects at the Showcase Event to an audience of industry professionals and peers. You'll be trained and supported to present with confidence.
When do applications open?+
Applications open on 1 July 2026. Until then, explore the program here and decide if AI Launch Pad is the right opportunity for you. Tap "Notify me" to be the first to know when registration goes live.
